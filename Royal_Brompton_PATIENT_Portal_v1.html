Common Issues & Solutions

Try these solutions first - they resolve most issues quickly

Quick Checks:

  • Check the power cable is firmly connected at both ends
  • Try a different power outlet
  • Check the power switch on the back of the device is ON
  • If using a power strip, ensure it's switched on
  • Look for any lights on the device - even dim ones

Still not working? This may be a device fault - please submit a support request below and mark it as urgent.

Try These Steps:

  • Loosen the straps - Over-tightening often causes more leaks
  • Check mask position - Should sit on the bridge of your nose, not too high or low
  • Clean the cushion - Wash daily with mild soap and water
  • Check for wear - Cushions should be replaced every 3-6 months
  • Facial hair? - Even stubble can cause leaks - try a different mask style

Need a replacement cushion? You can order one in the "Order" tab above.

Common Causes:

  • Dirty filter - Check and replace if grey/dirty (see "Order" tab)
  • Blocked air inlet - Ensure nothing is covering the air inlet on the device
  • Tubing position - Make sure tubing isn't kinked or twisted
  • Water in tubing - If using heated humidifier, condensation can cause gurgling - drain it
  • Mask vent - Some noise from mask vent holes is normal

High-pitched whistling or grinding? This isn't normal - please submit a support request.

What You Can Try:

  • Ramp feature - Check if your device has "ramp" mode - this starts at lower pressure
  • EPR/C-Flex - Some devices have pressure relief on exhale - check your settings menu
  • Mouth breathing? - If using nasal mask and breathing through mouth, you may need a chin strap or full face mask
  • Give it time - It can take 2-4 weeks to adjust to therapy

⚠️ Important: Do NOT adjust pressure settings yourself. If pressure feels wrong, request a clinician callback below - settings may need professional adjustment.

Solutions:

  • Increase humidifier setting - Turn up the humidity level on your device
  • Use heated tubing - Prevents condensation and improves humidity delivery
  • Room humidity - Consider a bedroom humidifier in dry months
  • Nasal saline spray - Use before bed (check with GP if unsure)
  • Water level - Ensure humidifier chamber has water

Need a new humidifier chamber? You can order one in the "Order" tab.

How to Reduce Marks:

  • Loosen the straps - Mask should be snug but not tight
  • Adjust headgear - May need repositioning for even pressure
  • Clean mask daily - Oils from skin can cause irritation
  • Try different size - May need a different size cushion
  • Moisturise - Use gentle, unscented moisturiser on contact areas (avoid inside nose)

Persistent sores or skin breakdown? Request a clinician callback - you may need a different mask style.

Common Error Messages:

  • "Check Tube" / "Tube Blocked" - Check tubing for kinks, blockages, or water
  • "Check Mask" - Ensure mask is properly connected to tubing
  • "Check Filter" - Replace filter (see "Order" tab)
  • "Check Water Chamber" - Ensure humidifier chamber is properly seated

Different error or persists after checks? Note down the exact error message and submit a support request below.
