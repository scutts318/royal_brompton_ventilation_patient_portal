# Royal Brompton Sleep & Ventilation Portal

**Patient and clinician portal for the Royal Brompton Hospital Sleep & Ventilation Department, GSTT**

A paired web-based portal system — one interface for patients, one for clinicians — designed to reduce unnecessary clinical contact, streamline equipment ordering, and improve self-management for patients on long-term home ventilation (NIV/CPAP).

---

## Background

The Royal Brompton Sleep & Ventilation service manages a large cohort of patients on long-term home respiratory support across South East England. Historically, routine equipment requests, troubleshooting queries, and consumables ordering required direct clinical contact — creating significant administrative burden on a specialist team.

This portal was designed to shift appropriate self-management to patients, while giving clinicians a structured view of patient equipment profiles, annual allowances, and order history.

---

## Patient Portal

A mobile-friendly patient-facing interface providing:

- **Equipment ordering** — patients can order prescribed consumables (masks, cushions, filters, tubing, humidifier chambers) within their annual allowance, with real-time quantity tracking
- **Troubleshooting guide** — step-by-step self-help for the most common device issues: power problems, mask leaks, device noise, pressure discomfort, dry nose/throat, skin irritation, and error messages
- **Support requests** — structured form for submitting technical issues, clinician callback requests, and equipment concerns, with urgent flagging for breathing difficulties or device failure
- **Request history** — full log of previous orders and support interactions with status tracking

---

## Clinician Portal

A clinician-facing companion interface providing:

- **Patient search and profile creation** — search existing patients or register new referrals with NHS number, diagnosis, and device type
- **Equipment profile configuration** — prescribe specific devices and masks (from ResMed, Philips, Löwenstein, Breas, Fisher & Paykel) with size selection and part-level detail
- **Annual allowance management** — set and track consumable allowances per patient
- **Order history review** — full audit trail of patient-initiated orders

## How to Use

Open `Royal_Brompton_PATIENT_Portal_v1.html` or `Royal_Brompton_CLINICIAN_Portal_v1.html` in any modern web browser. No installation or internet connection required.

> **Note:** This is a prototype/design demonstrator. Integration with Epic/MyChart and NHS login infrastructure would be required for live deployment. Patient data in the demo is entirely fictional.

---

## Clinical Context

This project was developed as part of a digital transformation initiative within the Royal Brompton Sleep & Ventilation department. It demonstrates a model for patient-initiated consumables management that could reduce clinical workload while improving access for patients across a geographically dispersed home ventilation cohort.
